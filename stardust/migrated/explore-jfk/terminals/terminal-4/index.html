Terminal 4 by the numbers

1.2M

The heaviest aircraft departing from T4, the Airbus A380, weighs 1.2 million pounds (that's over 600 tons)!

20M

Bags handled at T4 annually. That’s more than 2,000 bags an hour, or almost 40 bags a minute, every day. Whew!

75K

People never forget how you made them feel: On average, T4 employees say “Have a great flight!” to 75,000 passengers daily.

50%

Au revoir, adiós, arrivederci, sayonara: About 50% of our passengers are bound for international destinations.

135K

Get those miles in: T4 has more than 135,000 square feet of shops and restaurants. That’s a mile end-to-end — perfect for doing some cardio before your flight!
