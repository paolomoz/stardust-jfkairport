Terminal 1

Terminal 4

Terminal 1 serves several major international airlines, including Air France, Turkish Airlines, Lufthansa, Swiss and Korean Air. Travelers will find essential amenities, convenient dining, and duty-free shopping.

Terminal 5

Many airlines operate out of Terminal 4, including Delta, Avianca, Virgin Atlantic, Emirates, LATAM and more. Check out current wait times, as well as lounges available, shops and restaurants.

Terminal 7

Airlines that operate out of Terminal 5 are JetBlue, Cape Air, and Sun Country Airlines. Check out current wait times, as well as shops and restaurants available.
