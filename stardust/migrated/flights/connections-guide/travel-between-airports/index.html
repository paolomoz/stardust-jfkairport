Transferring Between Airports Guide

Connecting flights at another airport

Sometimes your trip may involve arriving at one New York airport and departing from another. For example, you might fly into LaGuardia (LGA) from St. Paul, Minnesota, and then depart from JFK on an international flight.

Transportation options when traveling between airports

For traveling between JFK and EWR:

  • Public transportation
  • Taxis
  • Ride sharing apps
  • Shuttle and van services
  • Limousine and private car services
