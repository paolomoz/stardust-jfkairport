Getting to and from JFK Airport via ride apps

Uber and Lyft are popular ride share apps available to get to and from JFK for your convenience. Follow the signs after baggage claim to find pickup areas prior to requesting a ride.

Ride app pick-up and drop-off locations

Ride app costs to and from JFK

Passengers can generally be picked up and dropped off in front of their terminals, but this may vary depending on the time of day and terminal. Check your app for the latest details on pick-up and drop-off locations.
