AirTrain is the best way to navigate JFK

Whether you're moving between terminals, leaving the airport via pick-up areas, or headed to public transit, rental cars, or a hotel shuttle, the AirTrain is the easiest way to get around JFK.

AirTrain connections

Between passenger terminals

The AirTrain takes passengers between terminals at JFK in under 10 minutes. This is a free, convenient service to access your connecting gate. Trains run every 5-10 minutes, but be sure to check monitors in the AirTrain

Learn more →

To and from NYC public transportation

You can access public transportation by taking the AirTrain to Jamaica or Howard Beach Station. The one-way AirTrain fare between JFK and these stations is $8.75. To learn more, visit our public transportation page.

Find and book parking →

To and from parking lots

You can easily access parking areas by taking the AirTrain. Each terminal is a short walk to its nearby short-term parking lot. Long-term parking is available near the Lefferts Boulevard Station.

Reserve a rental car →

To and from hotel shuttles

Heading to your hotel? JFK offers a designated hotel shuttle pickup and dropoff area. Take the AirTrain to Federal Circle via the Jamaica or Howard Beach AirTrain lines to access hotel shuttles.

To and from rental cars

JFK partners with most rental companies. If you are looking to pick up or drop off a rental car, go to Federal Circle Station. Take the AirTrain there using the Jamaica or Howard Beach lines or from any terminal.
